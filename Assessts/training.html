Facebook Developer Circles is back with its Developer Circles Training Courses. Interest Forms closing on 24 June 2019

What is Developer Circle Training Courses?

Developer Circles training courses are premium online training opportunities aimed at providing its members with more tools to continue growing their skillset and training themselves in core technologies.

What training courses are available?

This time there are three training courses to choose from:
  • OpenClassrooms: Front-End Web Development
  • DataCamp: Data Science
  • Udacity: Deep Learning Training Courses

How will Developer Circles Training Courses be provided?

Developer Circles Training Courses will be provided online with the help of number of partners.

I am interested. How can I get started?

Interested Students are required to fill Training Courses Interest Form. The last date to fill the form is 14 June.
